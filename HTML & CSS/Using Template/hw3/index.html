Platinum

An important resource for any Tenno

Platinum Converter

Platinum is a game resource that can be bought with real money and used in an ingame store for gear and cosmetics or traded with other players for goods and services. Being able to trade platinum currency with other players means that even those who do not spend any real world money in the game can still have access to premium cosmetics. The value of platinum is directly tied with the value of the US Dollar, so this conversion will be accurate unless the developers of Warframe themselves decide to change the price of their currency
